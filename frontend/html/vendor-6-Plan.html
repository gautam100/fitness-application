Membership Plans

Plan A

Conversion Membership

Plan Price
Percentage sharing plan in which 30% of sales amount of the seller will be shared by the site at the time of enrolment of the client.
Benefits
A large amount of space with no limitation of data will be provided to the sellers.
Website will manage and look about into the data provided by the sellers and the seller will be able to update and notify information and data according to their choice and preferences too.
Sellers can also add on to discount offers to the customers by providing additional offers to customers at their own end in addition to the website offers.
Website will give exclusive and extensive client detail information to the sellers and even the choicest of clients will be given regular updates about the sellers and the offers provided by them in this section.
Complete seller support system will be provided by site and preferential.
